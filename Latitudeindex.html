Summary of Analysis: Latitude vs. X

Here lies analysis of weather from randomly selected cities around the world. Included is the visualization of various aspects of weather with relation to the Equatorial proximity.

Weather Plot Observations

1. As expected, maximum temperature is noticeably higher in the center of our Latitude range, closest to the Equator.

2. There appears to be more humidity and Cloudiness north of the Equator, but it could be a limit of our sample.

3. Wind Speed near the Equator is less severe
